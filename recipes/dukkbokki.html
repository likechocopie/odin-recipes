Dukkbokki

Description

Dukkbokki is a popular street food made with rice cakes and is generally spicy.

Ingredients
  • rice cakes
  • green onions
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • optionally, fish cake, eggs
Steps
  1. make the sauce
  2. Throw rice cakes into sauce
  3. add ingredients
  4. cook
  5. Enjoy!
